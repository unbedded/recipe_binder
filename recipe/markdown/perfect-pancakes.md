# Perfect Pancakes

A family favorite for weekend mornings. These fluffy pancakes are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 TBL granulated sugar  
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup whole milk
- 2 large eggs, beaten
- 2 TBL melted butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Mix dry ingredients in large bowl - flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
2. In separate bowl, combine wet ingredients - milk, beaten eggs, melted butter, and vanilla
3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and fold together until just combined (don't overmix)
4. Let batter rest for 5 minutes while griddle heats to 375°F
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake onto hot, lightly greased griddle
6. Cook until bubbles form on surface and edges look set (about 2-3 minutes)
7. Flip and cook until golden brown on bottom (1-2 minutes more)
8. Serve immediately with butter and maple syrup

## Notes

- Don't overmix the batter - lumps are okay and will result in fluffier pancakes
- Test griddle temperature with a drop of water - it should sizzle and evaporate quickly
- Keep cooked pancakes warm in 200°F oven until ready to serve
- Makes about 12 medium pancakes, serves 4 people
