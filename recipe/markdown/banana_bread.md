## Banana Walnut Bread (2 cups mashed bananas)

**Ingredients:**

* **2 cups mashed bananas** (\~4 medium bananas)
* 1/2 cup (1 stick) melted butter
* 3/4 cup sugar (can reduce to 2/3 cup if bananas are very ripe)
* 2 large eggs (beaten)
* 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
* 1 1/4 tsp baking soda
* 1/4 tsp salt
* **2 cups all-purpose flour**
* **3/4 cup chopped walnuts**

---

**Instructions:**

1. Preheat oven to **350°F (175°C)**. Grease a 9×5 loaf pan (or 2 smaller pans).
2. In a large bowl, mash bananas until smooth. Stir in melted butter.
3. Mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
4. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over the mixture, stir in.
5. Add flour and fold gently until just combined.
6. Stir in walnuts (reserve a few to sprinkle on top if desired).
7. Pour batter into the pan.
8. Bake for **60–70 minutes**, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
9. Let cool 10 minutes in the pan, then transfer to a wire rack.

---

👉 This keeps the **same taste balance** as the 1.5 cup version, just a little richer in banana and with a nice walnut crunch.
