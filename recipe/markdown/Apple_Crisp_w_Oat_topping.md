# Apple Crisp with Oat Topping


*This easy apple crisp - https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/229088/apple-crisp-with-oat-topping/

- **Prep Time:** 20 mins
- **Cook Time:** 40 mins
- **Total Time:** 1 hr
- **Servings:** 4

---

## Ingredients
- 6 apples — peeled, cored, and sliced
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon, divided
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup old-fashioned oats
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup cold butter

---

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to **350°F (175°C)**. Gather all ingredients.
2. Toss apples with white sugar and ½ teaspoon cinnamon in a medium bowl. Transfer to a 9-inch square baking dish.
3. In a separate bowl, mix brown sugar, oats, flour, and 1 teaspoon cinnamon.
4. Cut in cold butter with two knives or a pastry blender until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
5. Spread topping evenly over apples and gently pat down.
6. Bake for about **40 minutes**, until golden brown and bubbling at the edges.
7. Serve warm and enjoy!

---

### Cook’s Note
For best flavor, use **high-quality cinnamon** from a spice merchant or bulk store — it makes a noticeable difference.
