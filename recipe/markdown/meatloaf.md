A hearty, flavorful meatloaf made with a mix of ground beef and pork, seasoned with herbs and spices, and topped with a tangy-sweet glaze.  

- **Prep Time:** ~20 minutes  
- **Cook Time:** 70–80 minutes  
- **Total Time:** ~1 hour 40 minutes  
- **Yield:** 2 loaves  

---

## Ingredients  

### Meatloaf
- 2 lbs ground beef (85% lean)  
- 2 lbs ground pork  
- 2 medium onions, finely chopped  
- 4 cloves garlic, minced  
- 2 cups breadcrumbs  
- 3 large eggs  
- 1 ½ cups milk  
- 4 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce  
- 6 tablespoons ketchup  
- 2 teaspoons salt  
- 1 teaspoon black pepper  
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme  
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika  
- ½ cup ketchup  (topping)
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar  (topping)
- 2 teaspoons yellow mustard  (topping)

---

## Instructions  
1. **Preheat oven:** Set oven to **350°F (175°C)**.  
2. **Mix meatloaf base:** In a large bowl, combine beef, pork, onions, garlic, breadcrumbs, eggs, milk, Worcestershire, ketchup, salt, pepper, thyme, and smoked paprika. Mix until just combined—do not overmix.  
3. **Shape loaves:** Divide mixture into two loaves. Place in loaf pans or on a parchment-lined baking sheet.  
4. **Prepare topping:** In a small bowl, mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard. Spread evenly over both loaves.  
5. **Bake:** Place in oven and bake for **70–80 minutes**, or until internal temperature reaches **160°F (71°C)**.  
6. **Rest & serve:** Let loaves rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
