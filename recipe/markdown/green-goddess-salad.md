# Green Goddess Salad

Vibrant salad with creamy herb dressing that's both refreshing and satisfying. Perfect for lunch or as a side dish.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 2 TBL fresh lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup fresh chives, chopped
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 2 TBL fresh dill, chopped
- 2 anchovy fillets (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 6 cups mixed greens (arugula, spinach, lettuce)
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup toasted pumpkin seeds

## Instructions

1. Make dressing: combine mayo, yogurt, lemon juice, and minced garlic in bowl
2. Add all fresh herbs and anchovy fillets (if using)
3. Whisk until smooth, season with salt and pepper
4. Refrigerate dressing for at least 30 minutes to develop flavors
5. In large salad bowl, combine mixed greens
6. Add cucumber, avocado, cherry tomatoes, and red onion
7. Drizzle with desired amount of dressing and toss gently
8. Top with toasted pumpkin seeds
9. Serve immediately

## Notes

- Dressing keeps refrigerated up to 5 days
- Add protein like grilled chicken or hard-boiled eggs for main dish
- Toast pumpkin seeds in dry skillet for 2-3 minutes for extra flavor
- Anchovy fillets add umami but can be omitted for vegetarian version
- Serves 4-6 as side dish, prep time 15 minutes
