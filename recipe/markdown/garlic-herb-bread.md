# Garlic Herb Bread

Crusty artisan bread with aromatic garlic and fresh herbs. Perfect companion to pasta dinners or soup.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups bread flour
- 1 tsp active dry yeast
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 TBL olive oil
- 1 cup warm water (110°F)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 TBL fresh rosemary, chopped
- 1 TBL fresh thyme, chopped
- 1 tsp coarse sea salt for topping

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, combine flour, yeast, and salt
2. Make well in center and add olive oil and warm water
3. Mix until shaggy dough forms, then knead 8-10 minutes until smooth
4. Add minced garlic and herbs during last 2 minutes of kneading
5. Place in oiled bowl, cover, let rise 1 hour until doubled
6. Punch down dough and shape into round loaf
7. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet, cover, rise 30 minutes
8. Preheat oven to 450°F with dutch oven inside
9. Score top with sharp knife, sprinkle with coarse salt
10. Bake covered 20 minutes, then uncovered 10-15 minutes until golden

## Notes

- Bread is done when internal temperature reaches 200°F
- For crispier crust, place pan of water in oven bottom
- Fresh herbs are key - dried herbs won't provide same flavor
- Makes 1 loaf, serves 8-10 people
- Best eaten same day but freezes well wrapped tightly
