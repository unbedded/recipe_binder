Chicken Pot Pie is my idea of comfort food! Tender chicken, vegetables, and gravy, baked into a flaky pastry crust. 

- **Prep Time:** 25 minutes  
- **Cook Time:** 40 minutes  
- **Total Time:** 1 hour 5 minutes  
- **Servings:** 6  

---

## Ingredients
- 1 box refrigerated pie crusts (14.1 oz), at room temp  
- ⅓ cup butter  
- ⅓ cup chopped onion (or 1.5 Tb dried onion)  
- ⅓ cup flour  
- ½ teaspoon salt  
- ¼ teaspoon pepper  
- 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning  
- 1 ¾ cups chicken broth/stock  
- ½ cup milk (2% recommended)  
- 2 ½ cups cooked, shredded chicken  
- 2 cups frozen mixed veggies, thawed  

---

## Instructions
1. **Cook and shred chicken.** Set aside.  
2. **Preheat oven to 425°F.** Press one pie crust into a 9-inch pie plate and set aside.  
3. **Cook onions:** In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add chopped onion and cook ~2 minutes, stirring often, until tender.  
4. **Make roux:** Whisk in flour, salt, pepper, and poultry seasoning. Mixture will become thick.  
5. **Add liquids:** Gradually stir in milk and broth. Cook until mixture thickens and becomes bubbly.  
6. **Add chicken & veggies:** Stir in shredded chicken and mixed veggies. Remove from heat.  
7. **Assemble pie:** Pour filling into prepared crust. Top with the second crust. Seal edges and cut several slits in the top crust to vent.  
8. **Bake 30–40 minutes,** until crust is golden brown. Cover edges with foil for the last 15–20 minutes to prevent over-browning.  
9. **Cool slightly:** Let pie rest ~5 minutes before slicing and serving.
