Easy to Mix- 

This keto strawberry shortcake features tasty biscuits topped with delicious layers of sweet strawberries and rich, creamy whipped cream.

Prep Time 30minutes mins

Cook Time 15minutes mins

Total Time 45minutes mins

Servings: [4](https://healthyrecipesblogs.com/low-carb-strawberry-shortcake/) cakes

Calories: 375kcal

Author: [Vered DeLeeuw](https://healthyrecipesblogs.com/about/)

Ingredients

For the biscuits:

- ▢ 2 large eggs
- ▢ 1/2 cup sour cream 
- ▢ 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ▢ 1 teaspoon [stevia glycerite](https://www.iherb.com/pr/now-foods-better-stevia-zero-calorie-liquid-sweetener-organic-glycerite-8-fl-oz-237-ml/861) (equals ⅓ cup sugar) (I used 1/2 Tbsp of real sugar- jy)
- ▢ 1/4 teaspoon salt
- ▢ 2 cups [blanched finely ground almond flour](https://www.target.com/p/bob-39-s-red-mill-gluten-free-super-fine-almond-flour-16oz/-/A-14777847) (not almond meal) (4 oz)
- ▢ 3.5 teaspoons baking powder (3 teasp (5 ml) = 1 Tbsp (15 ml))
    
- Instructions

Make the biscuits:

1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F. (preheat air fryer, 325F- jy) Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg, sour cream, vanilla, stevia, and salt. Using a rubber spatula, mix in the almond flour, then don’t forget to sift in the baking powder.

1. Let the mixture stand for 5-10 minutes while the almond flour absorbs the liquid.  The batter will get a bit stiffer and easier to scoop or drop in dollops onto the baking surface.

1. Using a large 4-tablespoon scoop, place 4 mounds of the dough on the prepared baking sheet. (Actually, you can make smaller mounds and more shortbreads if you’d like.) Very lightly flatten the tops, not too much or they'll be too flat to slice. (I didn’t flatten the tops at all, I just dropped dollops on to parchment paper and baked them in an airfryer. jy)

1. Bake (on 6-13-24, I baked them in air fryer, 325F for 12 to 15 min., then put in freezer on a cookie sheet for 10 min- putting in freezer immediately after baking for 10 minutes seemed to give a moist interior, but probably not necessary. jy.) until golden, in oven about 15-18 minutes. Turn the oven off, remove the biscuits, and place them in the fridge on paper towels to quickly chill them.
    
2. Chill on cookie sheet in freezer for 15 min., then store in fridge for up to 5 days.  Freeze for long term storage.  (You can warm up the shortcake -before slicing berries over the shortcake,- in air fryer at 275 for 5 to 10 min. jy)
