# Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Secretly healthy dessert that's incredibly rich and creamy. No one will guess the secret ingredient!

## Ingredients

- 3 large ripe avocados
- 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
- 2 TBL almond butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp sea salt
- 2-3 TBL almond milk (as needed)
- Fresh berries for garnish
- Dark chocolate shavings for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cut avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop flesh into food processor
2. Add cocoa powder, maple syrup, almond butter, vanilla, and salt
3. Process until completely smooth, about 2-3 minutes
4. Scrape down sides and process again if needed
5. Add almond milk 1 TBL at a time if mixture seems too thick
6. Taste and adjust sweetness with more maple syrup if desired
7. Transfer to serving glasses or bowls
8. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving
9. Garnish with fresh berries and chocolate shavings

## Notes

- Use very ripe avocados for best texture and sweetness
- Can be made up to 2 days ahead - cover tightly to prevent browning
- Add 1 TBL espresso powder for mocha version
- Naturally vegan and gluten-free
- Serves 4, prep time 10 minutes
