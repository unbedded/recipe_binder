Here's a simple and reliable **homemade pizza dough** recipe:

---

### 🍕 Basic Pizza Dough Recipe

#### **Ingredients** (for 2 medium pizzas):

- 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
- 1 ½ cups (360 ml) warm water (about 110°F / 45°C)
- 3 ½ cups (440 g) all-purpose flour (plus more for dusting)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
    ---

### 🥣 Instructions:

#### 1. **Activate the Yeast**

- In a large bowl, combine the **warm water**, **sugar**, and **yeast**.
    
- Let sit for about **5–10 minutes** until it becomes foamy.
    
#### 2. **Make the Dough**

- Add **olive oil**, **salt**, and **flour** gradually (1 cup at a time), stirring with a wooden spoon or using a mixer with a dough hook.
    

#### 3. **Knead the Dough**

- Knead on a floured surface for **8–10 minutes** (or 5 minutes in a mixer) until smooth and elastic.
    

#### 4. **Let It Rise**

- Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap.
    
- Let rise in a warm place for **1–1.5 hours** or until doubled in size.
### ✅ Tips:

- **Cold ferment** in the fridge overnight for deeper flavor.    
- You can freeze the dough after the first rise—wrap tightly and freeze up to 3 months.
    
#### 5. **Shape and Use**

- Punch down the dough. Divide in half for 2 pizzas.
    
- Roll out to desired shape and thickness.
    
- Add toppings and bake at **475°F (245°C)** for **12–15 minutes** or until crust is golden and crispy.

# Cooking
## 🔧 Settings That Differ by Pizza Type and Oven Mode**

|Feature / Setting|🍕 Pizza (Std Bake)|🍕 Pizza (Convection)|🥟 Calzone (Std Bake)|🥟 Calzone (Convection)|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|**Oven Temp**|475°F (245°C)|450–475°F (230–245°C)|425–450°F (220–230°C)|400–425°F (205–220°C)|
|**Bake Time**|12–15 min|9–13 min|16–20 min|14–18 min|
|**Steam Vents Needed**|❌ Not needed|❌ Not needed|✅ Yes (2–3 slits)|✅ Yes (2–3 slits)|
|**Crust Texture**|Crisp bottom, chewy|Crisper throughout|Firm crust, soft inside|Crisp bottom, drier edge|
|**Watch Points**|Rotate mid-bake|Avoid over-browning|Undercooked center|Edge drying, vent properly|
## 📊  General Comparison – Calzone vs Pizza**

|Feature|🍕 Pizza|🥟 Calzone|
|---|---|---|
|**Shape & Structure**|Open-faced, flat|Sealed, folded pocket|
|**Filling Moisture**|Evaporates easily|Trapped — avoid overly wet ingredients|
|**Steam Management**|Not required|Needs vents to release trapped steam|
|**Edge Handling**|No sealing required|Seal tightly with crimp or fork|
|**Finishing Touch**|Optional olive oil or cheese drizzle|Egg wash recommended for browning|
