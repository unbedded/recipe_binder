# Crispy Air-Fried Brussels Sprouts  

Smoky, crispy, and bright—these Brussels sprouts use bacon grease for flavor, with optional garlic, paprika, and a finishing splash of lemon or balsamic glaze.  

- **Prep Time:** ~10 minutes  
- **Cook Time:** 8–10 minutes  
- **Total Time:** ~20 minutes  
- **Servings:** 3–4  

---

## Ingredients
- 1 lb Brussels sprouts — main ingredient  
- 2–3 tbsp bacon grease — fat for flavor + crisping  
- ½–1 tsp salt — base seasoning (adjust for bacon fat)  
- ½ tsp black pepper — depth and balance  
- ½ tsp garlic powder **OR** 2–3 cloves crushed garlic — savory flavor (see tips)  
- ½ tsp smoked paprika — optional; adds warmth/smoky depth  
- 1–2 tsp lemon juice **OR** balsamic glaze — finish; brighten and balance bitterness  
- 2–3 strips crumbled bacon (optional) — extra crunch + flavor  

---

## Instructions
1. **Prep:** Wash sprouts, trim stems, and cut in half. Place in a microwave-safe dish with a splash of water. Microwave 2–3 minutes to soften slightly.  
2. **Garlic option:** If using crushed garlic, gently warm bacon grease in a pan and sauté garlic 30–60 seconds until fragrant. Remove garlic pieces and reserve. Use the garlic-infused grease in Step 3. *(Add reserved garlic back at the end in Step 5.)*  
3. **Season & coat:** Toss sprouts with warm bacon grease (or garlic-infused grease). Add salt, pepper, garlic powder (if using), and smoked paprika. Mix well to coat evenly.  
4. **Air fry:** Preheat air fryer to **375–400°F**. Cook sprouts 8–10 minutes, shaking halfway. Look for browned, crispy edges and tender centers.  
5. **Finish:** If reserved garlic was used, add it back now. Toss with lemon juice or balsamic glaze. Sprinkle with crumbled bacon if desired.  
6. **Serve:** Enjoy hot as a side dish—crispy, smoky, and bright.
