# Homemade Pizza Dough

Restaurant-quality pizza dough that's crispy outside and chewy inside. Make ahead for quick weeknight pizzas.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp active dry yeast
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 TBL olive oil
- 1 cup warm water (110°F)
- 1 tsp sugar
- Cornmeal for dusting

## Instructions

1. In small bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water, sprinkle yeast on top
2. Let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy (if no foam, yeast is dead)
3. In large bowl, combine flour and salt
4. Make well in center, add yeast mixture and olive oil
5. Stir until rough dough forms, then knead on floured surface 8-10 minutes
6. Dough should be smooth and elastic, slightly tacky but not sticky
7. Place in oiled bowl, cover with damp towel, rise 1 hour until doubled
8. Punch down and divide into 2 portions for thin crust or keep whole for thick
9. Roll out on cornmeal-dusted surface to desired thickness
10. Top as desired and bake at 475°F for 10-15 minutes until golden

## Notes

- For best flavor, let dough cold-rise in fridge overnight after step 7
- Dough can be frozen up to 3 months - thaw in fridge before using
- High heat is key for crispy crust - use pizza stone if available
- Don't overload with toppings or crust will be soggy
- Makes 2 thin crust or 1 thick crust pizza, serves 4-6
