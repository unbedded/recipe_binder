    Ricotta cheese (¼ cup)
    Mozzarella cheese (½ cup shredded)
    Toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, spinach, cooked sausage, onions, olives, etc.
    A pinch of salt, pepper, oregano, or red pepper flakes
    Queso Oaxaca (½ cup, shredded)
	Black beans (¼ cup, drained and rinsed)
	Cooked ground beef or chorizo (¼ cup)
	Sautéed onions + bell peppers + corn
	Jalapeños (optional, for heat)
	Cilantro + cumin + chili powder (small pinch for seasoning)

## Instructions

- **Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C):** Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to heat up.  
- **Roll out dough:** On a floured surface, roll each dough ball into an 8–10" circle.  
- **Add filling:** On one half of the dough, layer your cheeses and toppings. Leave about 1" edge clear for sealing.  
- **Fold and seal:** Fold the other half of the dough over the filling. Press edges to seal and crimp with a fork. Optionally, cut 2–3 small slits in the top for steam to escape.  
- **Brush with egg wash:** This helps create a golden, glossy crust.   **Egg wash =** 1 egg + 1 tbsp water, beaten  
- **Bake:** Transfer to the hot baking sheet or stone. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until golden brown.  
- **Cool slightly & serve:** Let rest 5 minutes before cutting.  
- Serving Suggestions- Serve with sour cream or salsa  - Olive oil or marinara for dipping (optional)
