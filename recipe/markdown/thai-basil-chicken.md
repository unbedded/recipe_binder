# Thai Basil Chicken (Pad Kra Pao)

Authentic Thai stir-fry with aromatic holy basil and ground chicken. Quick, spicy, and incredibly flavorful - a true weeknight winner.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground chicken
- 3 TBL vegetable oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 Thai chilies, sliced (adjust to taste)
- 2 TBL oyster sauce
- 1 TBL dark soy sauce
- 1 tsp light soy sauce
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 2 cups fresh Thai basil leaves
- 4 large eggs (for serving)
- Jasmine rice for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat wok or large skillet over high heat until smoking
2. Add 2 TBL oil and swirl to coat the pan
3. Add minced garlic and sliced chilies, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant
4. Add ground chicken, breaking it up with spatula, cook 3-4 minutes until browned
5. Add oyster sauce, both soy sauces, and brown sugar - stir to combine
6. Cook 2-3 minutes until chicken is fully cooked and sauce is absorbed
7. Remove from heat and stir in fresh basil leaves until wilted
8. In separate pan, fry eggs in remaining oil until crispy edges form
9. Serve over jasmine rice topped with fried egg

## Notes

- Thai holy basil (bai kra pao) is preferred but regular basil works too
- Don't skip the fried egg - it's traditional and adds richness
- Adjust chilies based on heat preference - start with less and add more
- Serves 4 people, ready in 15 minutes
