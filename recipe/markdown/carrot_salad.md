
## Ingredients
- 3 cups shredded carrots (~1 lb)  
- 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained  
- ½ cup chopped walnuts  
- ½ cup shredded sweetened coconut  
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon  
- ¼ cup raisins (soaked)  
- Pinch of salt  
- 3 tablespoons reserved pineapple juice  (Topping)
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or Greek yogurt  (Topping)
- 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup  (Topping)

---

## Directions
1. **Prep carrots:** Shred carrots and place in a large mixing bowl.  
2. **Add mix-ins:** Stir in pineapple, walnuts, coconut, raisins, cinnamon, and salt.  
3. **Make dressing:** In a small bowl, whisk together pineapple juice, mayo (or yogurt), and honey (or syrup).  
4. **Combine:** Pour dressing over carrot mixture and toss until evenly coated.  
5. **Chill & serve:** Refrigerate 30 minutes before serving for best flavor.
