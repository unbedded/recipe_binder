# Rhubarb–Strawberry–Blackberry Cobbler

*A rustic summer dessert with a tangy-sweet fruit base and a tender biscuit-style topping. Perfect with ice cream or whipped cream.*

- **Prep Time:** ~20 mins
- **Cook Time:** 35–40 mins
- **Total Time:** ~1 hr
- **Servings:** 6–8

---

## Ingredients

- 2 cups pre-sweetened rhubarb — Fruit Filling  
- 1 ½ cups strawberries, halved — Fruit Filling  
- 1 cup blackberries — Fruit Filling  
- 2 tablespoons sugar (optional) — Fruit Filling  
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch — Fruit Filling  
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice — Fruit Filling  
- Pinch of cinnamon (optional) — Fruit Filling  

- 1 cup all-purpose flour — Topping  
- 3 tablespoons sugar — Topping  
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder — Topping  
- ¼ teaspoon salt — Topping  
- 3 tablespoons butter, cold and cubed — Topping  
- ½ cup milk or buttermilk — Topping  
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract — Topping  
---

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to **375°F (190°C)**. Grease an 8×8-inch baking dish.
2. In a bowl, gently toss rhubarb, strawberries, and blackberries with cornstarch, lemon juice, and sugar (if needed). Pour into the prepared dish.
3. For the topping: whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in butter with a fork or your fingers until the mixture is crumbly.
4. Stir in milk and vanilla until just combined.
5. Drop spoonfuls of topping over fruit (gaps are fine).
6. Bake **35–40 minutes**, until topping is golden and fruit is bubbling at the edges.
7. Cool slightly before serving — filling will thicken as it sets.

---

### Serving Tip
Best enjoyed warm with **vanilla ice cream** or lightly sweetened whipped cream.
