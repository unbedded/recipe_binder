Homemade Cinnabon like Cinnamon rolls
- Prep time 30-45 min   
- cook time 25-30 min  
- total rising time 2:15 min


- **1 ½ cups** granulated sugar
- **3 Tbsp**. ground cinnamon
	1. Mix together sugar/cinnamon: set aside

- **1 cup** 110 degree water
- 2 packages of active dry yeast (or 2 TBS instant yeast)
- **1 tsp**. granulated sugar
	1. in warm water -  dissolve yeast & sugar ; set aside 5min

- **1 cup** warm milk
- **2/3 cups** granulated sugar
- **2/3 cup** melted butter
- **2 tsp.** salt
- **2 eggs-** slightly beaten
- **<8 cups** all-purpose flour (6-7 cups)
	1. In MIXER add milk & sugar & melted butter &  salt & eggs & yeast mixture
	2. Slowly add **4 cups** o flour to mixture. Beat until smooth. 
	3. Stir in enough remaining flour to form slightly stiff dough.
	4.  Place dough into a large greased bowl, cover and allow to rise in a warm place until 
	5. doubled in size. About 1-1 ½ hours. 
	6.  Punch down dough and let rest for 5 min.

- **2/3 cups** granulated sugar
- **½ cup** melted butter (for on rolled surface)
	1. Roll dough out onto floured surface in to a 15”x20” rectangle
	2. Brush ½ cup melted butter over dough; sprinkle cinnamon sugar evenly over dough.
	3. Tightly roll dough up and pinch edges together to seal.  Cut the roll into 12-15 slices using dental floss or thread

- **12'** dutch oven
- **1/3cp** sugar
- **1/3cp** butter
	1. Coat the bottom of a 12" dutch - 13”x9”x2” baking pan generously 
	2. Coat w/ lots butter 
	3. sprinkle with 1/3 cup of sugar
	4. allow to rise in warm place for about 45 min or just eyeball them.
    5. Bake 350 degree for 25-30 min or until golden brown.
## Frosting
- 8 oz. cream cheese
- 8 oz. butter
- 1 tsp. vanilla 
- 16 oz. powder sugar
	1. Beat together all ingredients together, spread on warm rolls.
