The amount of spice in this recipe is just right for my family (mild side)

- **Total Time:** Prep 35 min · Bake 30 min  
- **Yield:** 8 servings  

---

## Ingredients
- 1 ½ pounds ground beef  
- 2 cans (14 ½ oz each) stewed tomatoes  
- 1 medium onion, chopped  
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder  
- ½ teaspoon chili powder  
- ¼ teaspoon salt  
- ¼ teaspoon pepper  
- 10 flour tortillas (6-inch)  
- 3 cups (12 oz each) shredded cheddar-Monterey Jack or Colby-Jack cheese  
- 1 can (2 ¼ oz) sliced ripe olives, drained  

---

## Directions
1. **Cook meat:** In a skillet, brown ground beef; drain. Add tomatoes, onion, and seasonings. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes.  
2. **Layer tortillas & filling:** Arrange 5 tortillas in the bottom of a 13×9-inch baking dish, tearing as needed to fit. Cover with half the meat mixture, then half the cheese. Repeat layers with remaining tortillas, meat mixture, and cheese. Top with olives.  
3. **Bake:** Place in oven at **350°F (175°C)** and bake for 30 minutes, until heated through.  
4. **Rest & serve:** Let stand a few minutes before cutting and serving.
